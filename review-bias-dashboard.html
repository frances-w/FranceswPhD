Research question
How does review collection method (prompted vs self-motivated) systematically affect ratings and review content?
Empirical strategy
Identification
Compare four review collection channels: Brand, OTA, TripAdvisor requested (TA-RE), and self-motivated (TA-SM)
Control for hotel fixed effects and isolate variation driven by collection mechanism
Data structure
Multi-platform panel of hotel reviews across platforms and collection types
Model framework
Rating ~ Collection Method + Hotel Fixed Effect + ε
Rating, Sentiment and Length modeled as dependent variables across platforms
Results — rating differences
Average rating by channel
Review length comparison
Key insights
Prompted reviews (Brand, TA-RE) produce systematically higher ratings due to response bias
Self-motivated reviews capture extreme experiences → longer and more polarized
Platform comparisons without controlling for collection method are fundamentally biased
